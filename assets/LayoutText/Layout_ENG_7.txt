The Celtic Cross
This traditional layout is used for clarity on a specific issue as well as for general readings.  In the OSHO Zen Tarot, the positions of the cards have the following meaning:


1- The issue
2- Diminishing/ enhancing the issue, or clarifying/ obscuring the issue
3- The unconscious influences
4- The conscious influences
5- Old patterns, the old way
6- New patterns, moving into the new
7- Self: your feelings and attitudes about the issue
8- What you are attracting from the outside
9- Your desires/denials
10- Outcome/key
