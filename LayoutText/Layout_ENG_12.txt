Horseshoe Spread
Provides insights into a particular situation or question from multiple angles, including past influences, current challenges, and future opportunities.

Useful if you are looking for strategies on how to reach the desired outcome.  It’s not just a case of "This is the outcome – good luck!" You are given rich information about the obstacles and how to overcome them.

1. Past
2. Present
3. Future
4. Attitude towards question
5. Other influences
6. Obstacles
7. Likely outcome
